The work addresses lens ghosting those green/white dots caused by internal reflections by using IMU sensor data to detect ghost artifacts in live view, distinguish them from real objects via motion tracking, and remove them in real time with dynamic filtering. The patent was granted in 2024 and published globally in 2026, and the project blended optics, motion modeling, and computer vision to make the solution practical for everyday smartphone photography.
