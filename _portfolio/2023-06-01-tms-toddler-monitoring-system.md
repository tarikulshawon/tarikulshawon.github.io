Designed and implemented an AI-powered monitoring system for IoT surveillance devices to detect toddler activities and issue real-time alerts for hazardous behavior. The pipeline uses SlowFast for spatial semantics, YOLOv5 for object detection, and Deep SORT for robust multi-object tracking. OpenCV manages video streaming and preprocessing, with TensorRT optimizing edge inference for low latency.
