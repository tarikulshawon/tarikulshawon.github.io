Proposed a feature that replaces static notifications with AI-generated captions derived from image content, enabling more personalized and context-aware alerts. Evaluated vision-language models including Qwen 3.0, CLIP, and ViT-GPT2 across accuracy, latency, and resource utilization metrics. The proposal received headquarters R&D approval for development.
